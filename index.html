Hello, thanks for visiting our website

We are the premier commercial artist representative illustration agency for advertising and marketing visuals. Our illustrators' specialize in creating commercial storyboards, marketing and animated explainer video production, and any type of handcrafted illustration or design imaginable.

The skills and expertise of our Chicago-based illustration agency include storyboard artists, illustrators, editors, animators, graphic designers and photographers—all experienced advertising and marketing professionals.

As one of Chicago's leading animation studios, we also specialize in producing premium animatic storyboards, product demonstration videos and integrated media including sizzle reels and animated marketing videos for global brands and agencies. We understand great art has a spirit, a soul that stirs an emotion and that requires a great artist. Great art coupled with precise storytelling by expert illustrators helps sell your ideas. Let us help you bring your creative ideas to life!

If stock imagery is what you want, you’ve come to the wrong place. Everything we do at EdseyArt is custom created by experienced and talented commercial artists. Over the years, we’ve handpicked a wide selection of personalities and styles to take your ideas, give them style, and bring them to life.

Our Chicago based illustrators, graphic designers, and storyboard artists know how to put your product in the spotlight for serious impact.
